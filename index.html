Welcome to VISION

This innovative headwear is designed to empower the visually impaired by utilizing advanced visual recognition technology to detect objects in their environment. Paired with real-time audio feedback through an earpiece, it provides immediate information about the objects in front of them, helping users navigate their surroundings with greater confidence and independence. This seamless integration of technology ensures a more intuitive and reliable experience, making everyday activities easier and safer for those with visual impairments.

ESP32 is powerful, Wi-Fi and Bluetooth-enabled microcontroller designed for IoT and embedded applications.

ESP32-CAM, a compact ESP32-based module with a built-in camera, ideal for image processing and remote monitoring.

GPS Module is a device that receives satellite signals to determine precise location coordinates for navigation and tracking.

Bluetooth Earpiece, a wireless audio device that connects via Bluetooth for hands-free communication and audio playback.

App's Features

The app is expertly designed with a focus on both functionality and aesthetics. Its clean, modern interface ensures a seamless user experience, offering intuitive navigation that caters to users of all levels.

Sign In/Up Function

The Sign In/Up function allows users to easily create an account or log in to access personalized features and settings.

Location History Log

The app keeps a detailed record of past locations, offering users access to their complete Location History Log.

Precise Geolocation Mapping

With Precise Geolocation Mapping, the app ensures accurate, real-time tracking and visualization of the user's location.

Monitoring Battery Power

The app provides continuous monitoring of battery power, alerting users when their device needs attention.

Real-Time Distance Calculation

Real-Time Distance Calculation instantly determines and displays the distance between the user and their Guardian.
